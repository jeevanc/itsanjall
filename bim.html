BIM(Bachleor in Information Management)

Introduction

A BIM degree is an undergraduate academic program which extends over 8 semester(4 academic years).The BIM program is designed to equip graduates with the skills and attributes required to become an efficient and effective IT professionals to match up with the fast paced world of today.

Demand for IT professionals is extensively growing globally and Nepal is no exception. Hemce, the Faculty of Management(FOM),Tribhuvan University has developed the BIM program to cater to the need of this trend.

BIM program provides the students with mainfold future prospects for advance studies and rewarding employment.A BIM commands a vital role in the information management of any organizationthey are enrolled into. Being a Software Enginner with the managerial insights, s/he is expected to provide crucial support to all the functional areas as well as for strategic management.S/he, in particular, is expected to play a key role in the MIS function of the organization which of course a BIM student is more than qualified for.

In addition,BIM graduates can find jobs in almost every work arena.Thr diversity of opportunities that exists for them include Software Enginners, Programmers/Analysts,System desingers,Network managers,Database Managers and Trainers/Instructors among others.Four years of study of the comprehensive syllabus helps them to get straight entry into the Master Degree Programs in any of the recognized universities worldwide.

Curriular Structure

The BIM program will have 8 semester with 126 credit hours.Each semester will have duration of 6 months. The program comprises if the following course components:

  • Foundation Courses(24 credit hours)
  • Business Courser(30 credit hours)
  • Information Technology & Computer COurses(60 credit hours)
  • Elective Courses(6 credit hours)
  • Internship and Summer Project(6 credit hours)
    • Total(126 credit hours)
