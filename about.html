About Us

We are awesome TEAM

IT SANJAL is a complete web portal for those students who are looking out to make their career on currently the top course of the market BSc(CSIT) and BIM and those who are studying it.

Here you can find all the information you needed to know about the course if you are trying to choose your carrer and all the required academic course book if you are persuing this faculty.


As a student, it was difficult for us to find all notes, old questions or solutions related to the subject we were studying.

Thus, we thought why not we develop such a site so that the upcoming students don't have to face any problems as they can find all needed things in once site at one click.

  • To give a clear knowledge about Bsc(CSIT) and BIMs.
  • To provide a good platform to those students who are confused to choose about their career.
  • To provide a complete web solution to those students who are studying it.
