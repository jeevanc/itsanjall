B.Sc.CSIT(Bachleor in Computer Science and Information Technology)

Introduction

Bsc(CSIT) is a course composition of IT and computer courses of 4 years.For fulfilling the need of the job of the market in the field of IT profession,many elective courses have also been introduced. The program comprises computer science and ohter courses of 126 credit hours.

The program involves, in addition to conventional lectures, a great deal of practical and project works.The program develops the underlying principles of both Computer Science and Information Technology and shows how these principles can be applied to the real world problems. This program develops the skills that are essntinal for both computer professional and IT managers.

Eligibility for Admission

The candidate applying for B.Sc.CSIT programs must have:

  • Successfully completed tweleve-years schooling in science or its equivalent from any University,board or institution recognized by Tribhuvan University.
  • Appeared in the entrance test coducted by Institute of Science and Technology,Tribhuvan University(IOST_TU) and should have secured at least 45% marks in order to be eligible.
  • Secured at least 2nd division in the 10+2 or equivalent program.

Curricular Structure

The B.Sc.CSIT program will have the eight semester with 126 credit hours. Each semester shall have duration of 6 months.The program comprises of the following courses:

  • Computer Science Core Courses (75 credit hours)
  • Natural Science Elective Courses (6 credit hours)
  • Mathematcis Courses (12 credit hours)
  • English courses (3 credit hours)
  • Social Science and Management Courses (6 credit hours)
  • Computer Science Elective Courses (15 credit hours)
  • Internship/Project (9 credit hours)
    • Total(126 credit hours)
